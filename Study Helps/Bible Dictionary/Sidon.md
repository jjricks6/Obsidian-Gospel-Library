 [[Old Testament/Genesis/Genesis 10#^verse-15|Gen. 10:15]]. Firstborn of Canaan, and name of a city ([[Old Testament/Genesis/Genesis 10#^verse-19|Gen. 10:19]] and elsewhere in the Old Testament).
