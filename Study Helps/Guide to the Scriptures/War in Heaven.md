 The conflict that took place in the premortal life among the spirit children of God.

 Satan was cast out of heaven and into the earth, [[New Testament/Revelation/Revelation 12#^verse-4|Rev. 12:4, 7-9]].

 The devil and a third part of heaven were thrust down, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 29#^verse-36|D&C 29:36-37]].

 Lucifer rebelled against the Only Begotten Son, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 76#^verse-25|D&C 76:25-26]].

 Satan sought the glory of the Father and to destroy the agency of man, [[Pearl of Great Price/Moses/Moses 4#^verse-1|Moses 4:1-4]] ([[Old Testament/Isaiah/Isaiah 14#^verse-12|Isa. 14:12-15]]; [[Pearl of Great Price/Abraham/Abraham 3#^verse-27|Abr. 3:27-28]]).

 Those who followed God kept their first estate, came to earth, and received bodies, [[Pearl of Great Price/Abraham/Abraham 3#^verse-26|Abr. 3:26]].
