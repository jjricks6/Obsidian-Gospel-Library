## The tribe of Benjamin

 Jacob blessed Benjamin ([[Old Testament/Genesis/Genesis 49#^verse-27|Gen. 49:27]]). The descendants of Benjamin were a warlike race. Two important Benjamites were Saul, the first Israelite king ([[Old Testament/1 Samuel/1 Samuel 9#^verse-1|1 Sam. 9:1-2]]), and Paul, the New Testament Apostle ([[New Testament/Romans/Romans 11#^verse-1|Rom. 11:1]]).
