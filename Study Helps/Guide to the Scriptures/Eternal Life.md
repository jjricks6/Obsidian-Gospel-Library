 To live forever as families in God's presence ([[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 132#^verse-19|D&C 132:19-20, 24, 55]]). Eternal life is God's greatest gift to man.

 Thou hast the words of eternal life, [[New Testament/John/John 6#^verse-68|John 6:68]].

 This is life eternal, that they might know thee the only true God, and Jesus Christ, [[New Testament/John/John 17#^verse-3|John 17:3]] ([[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 132#^verse-24|D&C 132:24]]).

 Fight the good fight of faith, lay hold on eternal life, [[New Testament/1 Timothy/1 Timothy 6#^verse-12|1 Tim. 6:12]].

 People are free to choose liberty and eternal life, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 2#^verse-27|2 Ne. 2:27]] ([[Book of Mormon/Helaman/Helaman 14#^verse-31|Hel. 14:31]]).

 To be spiritually minded is life eternal, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 9#^verse-39|2 Ne. 9:39]].

 Then are ye in the narrow path that leads to eternal life, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 31#^verse-17|2 Ne. 31:17-20]].

 To believe in Christ and endure to the end is life eternal, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 33#^verse-4|2 Ne. 33:4]] ([[Book of Mormon/3 Nephi/3 Nephi 15#^verse-9|3 Ne. 15:9]]).

 He that hath eternal life is rich, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 6#^verse-7|D&C 6:7]] ([[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 11#^verse-7|D&C 11:7]]).

 Eternal life is the greatest of all the gifts of God, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 14#^verse-7|D&C 14:7]] ([[New Testament/Romans/Romans 6#^verse-23|Rom. 6:23]]).

 The righteous will receive peace in this world, and eternal life in the world to come, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 59#^verse-23|D&C 59:23]].

 Those who endure to the end shall have a crown of eternal life, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 66#^verse-12|D&C 66:12]] ([[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 75#^verse-5|D&C 75:5]]).

 All who die without the gospel who would have received it had they lived are heirs of the celestial kingdom, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 137#^verse-7|D&C 137:7-9]].

 God's work and glory is to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man, [[Pearl of Great Price/Moses/Moses 1#^verse-39|Moses 1:39]].

 God gives eternal life to the obedient, [[Pearl of Great Price/Moses/Moses 5#^verse-11|Moses 5:11]].
