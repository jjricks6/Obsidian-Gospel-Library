 In the Old Testament, a son of Jacob and Zilpah, Leah's handmaiden ([[Old Testament/Genesis/Genesis 30#^verse-12|Gen. 30:12-13]]).

## The tribe of Asher

 Jacob blessed Asher ([[Old Testament/Genesis/Genesis 49#^verse-20|Gen. 49:20]]), and Moses blessed the descendants of Asher ([[Old Testament/Deuteronomy/Deuteronomy 33#^verse-1|Deut. 33:1, 24-29]]). These descendants were called "mighty men of valour" ([[Old Testament/1 Chronicles/1 Chronicles 7#^verse-40|1 Chr. 7:40]]).
