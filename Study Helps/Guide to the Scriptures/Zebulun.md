## The tribe of Zebulun

 Jacob blessed the tribe of Zebulun ([[Old Testament/Genesis/Genesis 49#^verse-13|Gen. 49:13]]). The tribe of Zebulun joined with Deborah and Barak in fighting against the enemies of Israel ([[Old Testament/Judges/Judges 4#^verse-4|Judg. 4:4-6, 10]]). They also joined with Gideon in fighting the Midianites ([[Old Testament/Judges/Judges 6#^verse-33|Judg. 6:33-35]]).
