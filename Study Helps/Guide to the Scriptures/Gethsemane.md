 A garden spoken of in the New Testament as being near the Mount of Olives. In Aramaic, the word gethsemane means "olive press." Jesus went to the garden on the night that Judas betrayed Him. There He prayed and suffered in Gethsemane for the sins of mankind ([[New Testament/Matthew/Matthew 26#^verse-36|Matt. 26:36, 39]]; [[New Testament/Mark/Mark 14#^verse-32|Mark 14:32]]; [[New Testament/John/John 18#^verse-1|John 18:1]]; [[Book of Mormon/Alma/Alma 21#^verse-9|Alma 21:9]]; [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 19#^verse-15|D&C 19:15-19]]).
