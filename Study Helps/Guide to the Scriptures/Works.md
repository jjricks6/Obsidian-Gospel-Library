 A person's actions, whether good or bad. Each person will be judged by his own works.

 The Lord will render to every man according to his works, [[Old Testament/Proverbs/Proverbs 24#^verse-12|Prov. 24:12]].

 Let your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works, [[New Testament/Matthew/Matthew 5#^verse-16|Matt. 5:16]] ([[Book of Mormon/3 Nephi/3 Nephi 12#^verse-16|3 Ne. 12:16]]).

 He that doeth the will of my Father shall enter into the kingdom of heaven, [[New Testament/Matthew/Matthew 7#^verse-21|Matt. 7:21]].

 Faith without works is dead, [[New Testament/James/James 2#^verse-14|James 2:14-26]].

 They must be judged of their works, [[Book of Mormon/1 Nephi/1 Nephi 15#^verse-32|1 Ne. 15:32]] ([[Book of Mormon/Mosiah/Mosiah 3#^verse-24|Mosiah 3:24]]).

 We know that it is by grace that we are saved, after all we can do, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 25#^verse-23|2 Ne. 25:23]].

 Teach them to never be weary of good works, [[Book of Mormon/Alma/Alma 37#^verse-34|Alma 37:34]].

 It is requisite that men should be judged according to their works, [[Book of Mormon/Alma/Alma 41#^verse-3|Alma 41:3]].

 By their works ye shall know them, [[Book of Mormon/Moroni/Moroni 7#^verse-5|Moro. 7:5]] ([[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 18#^verse-38|D&C 18:38]]).

 I, the Lord, will judge all men according to their works, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 137#^verse-9|D&C 137:9]].
