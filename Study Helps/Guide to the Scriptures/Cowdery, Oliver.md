 The second elder of the restored Church and one of the Three Witnesses to the divine origin and truthfulness of the Book of Mormon. He acted as scribe while Joseph Smith translated the Book of Mormon from the gold plates ([[Pearl of Great Price/Joseph Smith—History#^verse-66|JS—H 1:66-68]]).

 Received a witness of the truth of the translation of the Book of Mormon, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 6#^verse-17|D&C 6:17, 22-24]].

 Was ordained by John the Baptist, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 13|D&C 13]] ([[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 27#^verse-8|D&C 27:8]]; [[Pearl of Great Price/Joseph Smith—History#^verse-68|JS—H 1:68-73]], see [[Pearl of Great Price/Joseph Smith—History|note to verse 71]]).

 After you have obtained faith and have seen them with your eyes, ye shall testify that you have seen them, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 17#^verse-3|D&C 17:3, 5]].

 I have manifested unto you, by my Spirit, that the things which you have written are true, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 18#^verse-2|D&C 18:2]].

 Was appointed and ordained one of the stewards over the revelations, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 70#^verse-3|D&C 70:3]].

 Received the keys of the priesthood with Joseph Smith, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 110|D&C 110]].
