 In the New Testament, a trusted Greek convert who traveled to Jerusalem with Paul and later became a missionary ([[New Testament/Galations/Galations 2#^verse-1|Gal. 2:1-4]]; [[New Testament/2 Timothy/2 Timothy 4#^verse-10|2 Tim. 4:10]]). Titus delivered Paul's first epistle to the Saints at Corinth ([[New Testament/2 Corinthians/2 Corinthians 7#^verse-5|2 Cor. 7:5-8, 13-15]]).
