 See also [[Study Helps/Guide to the Scriptures/Testimony|Testimony]]

 To bear witness by the power of the Holy Ghost; to make a solemn declaration of truth based on personal knowledge or belief.

 The Comforter shall testify of me, [[New Testament/John/John 15#^verse-26|John 15:26]].

 He commanded us to preach and to testify, [[New Testament/Acts/Acts 10#^verse-42|Acts 10:42]].

 Three witnesses shall testify to the truth, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 27#^verse-12|2 Ne. 27:12]].

 The power of the Holy Ghost carrieth it unto the hearts of the children of men, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 33#^verse-1|2 Ne. 33:1]].

 The scriptures testify of Christ, [[Book of Mormon/Jacob/Jacob 7#^verse-10|Jacob 7:10-11]] ([[New Testament/John/John 5#^verse-39|John 5:39]]).

 I testify unto you that I do know that these things whereof I have spoken are true, [[Book of Mormon/Alma/Alma 5#^verse-45|Alma 5:45]] ([[Book of Mormon/Alma/Alma 34#^verse-8|Alma 34:8]]).

 Ye shall testify of them by the power of God, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 17#^verse-3|D&C 17:3-5]].

 That which the Spirit testifies unto you, ye should do, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 46#^verse-7|D&C 46:7]].

 I sent you out to testify and warn, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 88#^verse-81|D&C 88:81]].
