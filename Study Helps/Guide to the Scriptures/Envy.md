 According to the scriptures, to desire something that belongs to another is wrong.

 The patriarchs, moved with envy, sold Joseph into Egypt, [[New Testament/Acts/Acts 7#^verse-9|Acts 7:9]].

 Charity envieth not, [[New Testament/1 Corinthians/1 Corinthians 13#^verse-4|1 Cor. 13:4]] ([[Book of Mormon/Moroni/Moroni 7#^verse-45|Moro. 7:45]]).

 Envy comes of pride, [[New Testament/1 Timothy/1 Timothy 6#^verse-4|1 Tim. 6:4]].

 Where envy is, there is confusion and every evil work, [[New Testament/James/James 3#^verse-16|James 3:16]].

 The Lord commanded men that they should not envy, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 26#^verse-32|2 Ne. 26:32]].

 There was no envy among the people of Nephi, [[Book of Mormon/4 Nephi#^verse-15|4 Ne. 1:15-18]].

 The envy and wrath of man have been my common lot all the days of my life, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 127#^verse-2|D&C 127:2]].
