 An Old Testament prophet who prophesied around 520 B.C. He lived at the same time as the prophet Haggai ([[Old Testament/Ezra/Ezra 5#^verse-1|Ezra 5:1]]; [[Old Testament/Ezra/Ezra 6#^verse-14|6:14]]).

## The book of Zechariah

 The book is noted for its prophecies of Christ's mortal ministry and His Second Coming ([[Old Testament/Zechariah/Zechariah 9#^verse-9|Zech. 9:9]]; [[Old Testament/Zechariah/Zechariah 11#^verse-12|11:12-13]]; [[Old Testament/Zechariah/Zechariah 12#^verse-10|12:10]]; [[Old Testament/Zechariah/Zechariah 13#^verse-6|13:6]]). [[Old Testament/Zechariah/Zechariah 1|Chapters 1-8]] contain a series of visions of the future of God's people. [[Old Testament/Zechariah/Zechariah 9|Chapters 9-14]] contain visions about the Messiah, the last days, the gathering of Israel, the final great war, and the Second Coming.
