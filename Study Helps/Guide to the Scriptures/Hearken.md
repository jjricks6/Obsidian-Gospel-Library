 To hear and obey the voice or teachings of the Lord.

 The Lord will raise up a prophet like Moses, and the people should hearken to him, [[Old Testament/Deuteronomy/Deuteronomy 18#^verse-15|Deut. 18:15]].

 To hearken is better than the fat of the rams of sacrifice, [[Old Testament/1 Samuel/1 Samuel 15#^verse-20|1 Sam. 15:20-23]].

 We have not hearkened unto the Lord's servants, the prophets, [[Old Testament/Daniel/Daniel 9#^verse-6|Dan. 9:6]].

 The righteous who hearken unto the words of the prophets shall not perish, [[Book of Mormon/2 Nephi/2 Nephi 26#^verse-8|2 Ne. 26:8]].

 If you will not hearken to the voice of the good shepherd, you are not his sheep, [[Book of Mormon/Alma/Alma 5#^verse-38|Alma 5:38]] ([[Book of Mormon/Helaman/Helaman 7#^verse-18|Hel. 7:18]]).

 Hearken, O ye people of my church, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 1#^verse-1|D&C 1:1]].

 Those who hearken to the voice of the Spirit are enlightened and come to the Father, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 84#^verse-46|D&C 84:46-47]].

 They were slow to hearken to the Lord; therefore, he is slow to hearken to their prayers, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 101#^verse-7|D&C 101:7-9]].

 Those who do not hearken to the commandments are chastened, [[Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants/Doctrine and Covenants 103#^verse-4|D&C 103:4]] ([[Pearl of Great Price/Moses/Moses 4#^verse-4|Moses 4:4]]).
