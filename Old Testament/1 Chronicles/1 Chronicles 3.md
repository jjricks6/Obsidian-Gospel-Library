### Chapter 3

[[Old Testament/1 Chronicles/1 Chronicles 2|Previous Entry]]  ||  [[Old Testament/1 Chronicles/1 Chronicles 4|Next Entry]]

*David's sons are named—The successors of Solomon to Jeconiah and beyond are listed.*

**1**  Now these were the [[#^note-1a|sons]] of David, which were born unto him in Hebron; the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess: ^verse-1

**2**  The third, [[#^note-2a|Absalom]] the son of Maachah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the fourth, [[#^note-2b|Adonijah]] the son of Haggith: ^verse-2

**3**  The fifth, Shephatiah of Abital: the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife. ^verse-3

**4**  These six were born unto him in Hebron; and there he [[#^note-4a|reigned]] seven years and six months: and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty and three years. ^verse-4

**5**  And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and [[#^note-5a|Solomon]], four, of [[#^note-5b|Bath-shua]] the daughter of [[#^note-5c|Ammiel]]: ^verse-5

**6**  Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet, ^verse-6

**7**  And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia, ^verse-7

**8**  And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine. ^verse-8

**9**  These were all the sons of David, beside the sons of the [[#^note-9a|concubines]], and [[#^note-9b|Tamar]] their sister. ^verse-9

**10**  ¶ And Solomon's son was [[#^note-10a|Rehoboam]], Abia his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son, ^verse-10

**11**  Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son, ^verse-11

**12**  Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son, ^verse-12

**13**  Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son, ^verse-13

**14**  Amon his son, Josiah his son. ^verse-14

**15**  And the sons of Josiah were, the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third [[#^note-15a|Zedekiah]], the fourth [[#^note-15b|Shallum]]. ^verse-15

**16**  And the sons of [[#^note-16a|Jehoiakim]]: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son. ^verse-16

**17**  ¶ And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, Salathiel his son, ^verse-17

**18**  Malchiram also, and Pedaiah, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah. ^verse-18

**19**  And the sons of Pedaiah were, [[#^note-19a|Zerubbabel]], and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister: ^verse-19

**20**  And Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab-hesed, five. ^verse-20

**21**  And the sons of Hananiah; Pelatiah, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shechaniah. ^verse-21

**22**  And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six. ^verse-22

**23**  And the sons of Neariah; Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three. ^verse-23

**24**  And the sons of Elioenai were, Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Dalaiah, and Anani, seven. ^verse-24
